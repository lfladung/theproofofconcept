# Gems Inspired by Hades 2 Boons

Translated into your 7 infusion families: **Edge, Flow, Mass, Echo, Anchor, Phase, Surge**.
Resource-gain effects, Chaos timer effects, and pure elemental flavor have been dropped — focus is on the underlying mechanics.

## Rarity System

Every gem rolls at one of five rarities. The same gem at higher rarity has stronger numbers (and sometimes an added mechanic at Legendary).

| Rarity | Color | Tier Multiplier (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Common | Gray | 1.0× |
| Uncommon | Green | 1.5× |
| Rare | Blue | 2.0× |
| Epic | Purple | 3.0× |
| Legendary | Orange | 4.0× *(or + new mechanic)* |

In gem text below, slash notation shows the value at each tier in order: **Common / Uncommon / Rare / Epic / Legendary**.

## Bombs and Bomb Zones

Bomb has absorbed the role of Cast. A Bomb can either explode on impact or place a **bomb zone** — a persistent area that ticks damage and hosts effects (debuffs, pulls, lightning, etc.). When a gem says "bomb zone" it refers to this placed-area form. When it says "Bomb hits" it refers to the impact damage. **Surge Bomb** = the charged/held variant, which is what most "Surge" gems modify.

## New Mechanics to Consider Adding

These are mechanics from the source boons that don't appear in the project notes. Listed roughly in order of how reusable they are.

### High-leverage additions (many gems depend on these)

- **Status effects with shared semantics.** A small palette of debuffs that gems can stack onto and reference. Recommended minimum: **Wounds/Bleed** (DoT + bonus damage hooks), **Slow** (move/attack speed reduction), **Mark** (next hit is special), **Daze/Stun** (skip an action).
- **Critical hits.** Already implied in your Sword design. Make sure crits are a real damage-multiplier system that gems can hook into (extra crit chance, conditional crits, crit-on-trigger).
- **Impervious / i-frames as a buff.** Distinct from dodge. A short window where damage is fully blocked.
- **Deflect.** A short window where projectiles bounce back. Cheap to build if you already have projectile-vs-shield logic.
- **Damage cap per hit.** A defensive ceiling — "no single hit deals more than X." Anchor identity hinges on this.
- **Bomb zones as a primitive.** Already decided. Make sure the underlying system supports: tick cadence, foes-inside queries, and modifier hooks.
- **Telegraphed falling/dropping projectiles.** Foe-targeted attacks that drop from above with a brief warning. Re-skinnable as blades, lightning, meteors. Powers a whole Echo branch.
- **Chain-lightning / bouncing procs.** A proc that hops between targets with optional bounce-off-self.
- **Auto-seeking projectiles.** Procs that fire homing shots on hit. Lets gems "add a second weapon" without another input.
- **In-run gem rarity upgrades.** Need a way to promote a gem's rarity mid-run (Bridal Glow, Steady Growth depend on this).

### Medium-leverage (useful for several gems)

- **Death Defiance / extra lives.** Consumable revives that refresh each run.
- **Knockback poise / super-armor.** Some hits don't stagger you. Required for any "tank" build to feel good.
- **Resource-as-shield.** Magick-style resource that can also absorb damage.
- **Stand-in-your-own-zone bonus.** Effects that reward being inside the AoE you placed.
- **Pull / vacuum effects.** Drag foes toward a point.
- **Time slow (bullet-time).** Slows everything else for a window. Phase identity defining.
- **Aimable bomb placement.** Hold to aim where Bomb lands. May already partly exist in your Bomb design.
- **Damage-on-spawn.** Effects that hit foes as they enter the encounter.
- **Execute thresholds.** Foes shatter / die instantly below X% HP under certain conditions.
- **Instant-kill chance on first hit.** Trash-mob sweep button. Gates hard with conditions.
- **Temporary minions from kills.** Slain foes briefly fight for you. Creates "snowball" feel.
- **Polymorph / Morph.** Turn foes into harmless creatures.
- **Trail-on-sprint.** Sprinting leaves a damaging or slowing trail.
- **Per-hit projectile slowdown.** Slow enemy projectiles so dodging is a skill check.
- **Mark + jump.** A target is Marked; killing or critting it transfers the Mark.

### Lower-leverage (cool but optional)

- **Aerial slam / leap-and-crash.** Big visual ability that exits and re-enters the arena.
- **Temporary alt-form / power state.** A 5-second "become the boss" mode. A great capstone gem.
- **Orbiting/homing companion projectiles.** Heartthrobs — orbs that float around you and home on foes.
- **Summoned hazards.** Spawn destructible objects that explode on contact (Soul Urns).
- **Trailing projectile (Beach Ball).** A sprint-charged orb that surges forward on release.
- **Variable-cost overcharge.** Optional resource investment to upgrade a single Surge cast in real time.
- **Delayed self-damage trade.** Effects that hurt you in exchange for big payoffs (Hazard Boom).
- **Armor-shatter explosion.** Losing all armor triggers a one-time blast.
- **Encounter-timer burst.** A burst of damage to a boss after N seconds in the fight.
- **Trigger-cascade.** Inflicting a status occasionally also triggers all "after you take damage" effects.
- **Family-diversity scaling.** Damage scales with the number of distinct gem families you carry.
- **Mystery HP / hidden stats.** Gain extra HP but hide your exact current value. Risk-flavored Anchor.

## Cuts and Why

Dropped per your instruction:

- **Pure resource generators** (Glamour Gain, Lucid Gain, Tranquil Gain, Cardio Gain, Flood Gain, Ionic Gain, Born Gain, Tough Gain, Burnt Offering, Plentiful Forage, Quick Buck, Buried Treasure, Bottomless Drink, Grisly Gain, Aether Font effects). Pacing tools, not build-defining.
- **Chaos timer effects** (Pauper's, Atrophic, Excruciating, Maimed, Flayed, Caustic, etc.) and the timed-buff side of Chaos boons. The interesting half — the permanent payoff — is folded into the families above.
- **Pure elemental flavor** (Scorch as fire, Freeze as water, Blitz as air). The mechanics behind them — DoT, slow, chain — are listed family-agnostic. Re-skin to taste.
- **Cross-Olympian Aether boons** (Freezer Burn, Scalding Vapor, Thermal Dynamics, Killer Current, Romantic Spark) that *only* matter when two specific gods are stacked. Not portable to a 7-family system.
- **Keepsake / Aspect / Arcana scalers** (Cherished Heirloom, Premium Service) that depend on Hades 2's specific meta-progression layers.
