The Proof of Concept
Humanity has gathered at the border of a pit that is seemingly endless. Strange and wonderful creatures make their way towards the surface, where adventurers fight and collect valuable resources. These can be rare metals, chemicals, hides of animals that defy natural laws.

They organize teams to enter the pit to seek out treasure and artifacts that no human could ever hope to recreate. But also to discover the origin of these things.

As they go deeper into the pit, they find ruins that house strange machines that work in abstract ways, powering themselves without any energy source.

The beings in the pit often come out differently, with different attributes, but also incomplete, as if something gave up halfway through their creation.

Deeper, the laws of physics change, as if it was a playground, a sandbox for a child’s play things. Floating islands, rivers flowing against gravity, bizarre plant life that shouldn’t be able to survive so far from the sun.

The pit is where reality is:
    •    Trying new forms
    •    Testing new rules
    •    Exploring possibilities
Not because it wants to…but because it can’t stop.

When anything leaves the pit:
    •    Weapons lose unique effects
    •    Materials degrade into base components
    •    Creatures collapse or simplify
Encourage use it before you lose it.

The pit is where reality is still forming.
The surface is where reality has stabilized

Every creature exists as a “concept” that becomes more complete the deeper you go.
    •    Near surface → partial manifestation
    •    Mid-depth → functional form
    •    Deep layers → fully realized (or over-realized)

Deeper creatures might:
    •    Combine traits from multiple species
    •    Show hybridization
Because:
Concept is merging ideas to see what works

“You don’t find new creatures below.
You find what they were meant to be.”
